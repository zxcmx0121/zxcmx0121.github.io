The application of LLMs in Traffic

Consultation and Q&A Assistant

LLM can provide intelligent Q&A help in the field of transport, and can be used in scenarios such as promoting general knowledge of the transport industry, assisting with consultation, and assisting with Q&A services such as 12328 and other transport hotlines, as well as generating transport solutions and consulting on existing solutions.

Transportation planning

Through the training and configuration of a large number of existing transportation planning and design results, the design work of the transportation industry can be carried out, relevant planning reports can be generated, and drawings of each stage can be further generated. LLM can be used to analyze reports from environmental groups and society’s attitudes toward sustainable transportation, providing city planners with insights into sustainable travel patterns. This can help urban planning become more aligned with society’s sustainability goals and provide design efficiencies.

Traffic analysis and management

LLM is used in the transportation industry to process large amounts of traffic data, including real-time traffic flow, road conditions, and historical data. By analyzing this information, it is possible to predict future traffic congestion, optimize traffic flow, and provide real-time navigation suggestions. The model can assist in traffic operation monitoring, such as image and video analysis, natural language processing, and speech recognition.

Traffic management in emergency situations

Use LLM to analyze social media and news reports to quickly understand the impact of emergency situations such as traffic accidents and natural disasters. At the same time, try to use satellite images or other types of data to identify the surrounding environment of transportation facilities and grasp the real-time situation on site. It can also use historical data and other information to identify patterns or trends in hazard impacts. This helps traffic managers respond more promptly and take measures to reduce traffic pressure.

Autonomous vehicle communication

LLM can be used by drivers to control vehicle navigation, driving habits and other functions. This could further improve communication between self-driving vehicles, promoting more efficient traffic flow. By understanding the language and instructions between vehicles, the risk of traffic accidents can be reduced and the safety of the overall transportation system improved.
